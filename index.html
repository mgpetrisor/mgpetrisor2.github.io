Nigel Marven

Biography

Nigel Marven studied at Bristol University and began a career with wildlife upon joining the BBC Natural History Unit. After working alongside Sir David Attenborough for 12 years he presented episodes of Walking with Dinosaurs and then Prehistoric Park before forming his own production company in 2003.

Qoute

You can’t go picking and choosing whether or not to save an animal – where on earth would you stop?
- Nigel Marven, Oct 22, 2010
